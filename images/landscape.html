Landscape

Among all types of photography, my favorite is still lansdcepe photography since I am very interested in outdoor adventures and intrigued by documenting breath-taking natural scenes. In 2019 May, I went to Hawaii with my mom for two weeks. We stayed on the island Ouhu for about one week and saw lots of beautiful scenes.

The sunrise and sunset moments are magic on Oahu island. We went to a lighthouse near the edge of island to see sunrise at 5am. The rising sun, sea, and lighthouse creates a dreamy tone and composition. I also really like the silhouette of a man who is carrying a surfing board, which creates a tone of “summertime sadness” on the background of a beautiful sunset.

Some of the photos were shot in Costco Rica, which I highly recommend for tourism. Costa Rica is the pearl of Central America, which covers various types of landforms, such as volcanos, rainforests, lagoons, oceans, and so forth. The diversity of scenery provides tourists with a wide range of choices. Besides, Costco Rica is also a popular travel destination for solo female travellers because of its relatively high safety in America.
